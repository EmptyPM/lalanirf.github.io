What I am Doing

I am a master’s student in the department of Materials Science and Engineering at National Chung Hsing University, Taiwan. For my master’s degree I carried out research on “The biocompatibility and antimicrobial activity of Copper(II) oxide hybridized with Nano silicate platelets”. In this research, we cultivated Cupric Oxide nanoparticles on Nano silicate platelets which are obtained from exfoliation of sodium montmorillonite clays. The main aim of this project was to improve the antimicrobial activity by reducing the aggregation of nanoparticles and reducing the cytotoxicity towards mammalian cells with the effect of Nano silicate platelets

“The biocompatibility and antimicrobial activity of Copper(II) oxide hybridized with Nano silicate platelets”.

In this research we cultivated Cupric Oxide nanoparticles on Nano silicate platelets which are obtained from exfoliation of sodium montmorillonite clays. Nano silicate platelets were first invented by Professor J.J.Lin, a professor at National Chung Hsing University. The main aim of this project was to improve the antimicrobial activity by reducing the aggregation of nanoparticles and reduce the cytotoxicity towards mammalian cells with the effect of Nano silicate platelets.

Supervisors: Dr. B.S. Dassanayake and Dr. B.M.K. Pemasiri
"Assessment of combined effects of rice husk ash and nylon fiber on engineering properties of cement mortar"

Focus of the research was to improve the performance of conventional cement mortar by incorporating Rice husk ash and Nylon fiber. The effects of Rice husk ash and Nylon fiber on the physical properties of cement mortar were observed.

Supervisors: Dr. V.A. Seneviratne
"Corrosion inhibition techniques and their development"

A deep study on the Corrosion inhibition techniques and the current development in the field. The written and oral materials relevant to the study were awarded at the undergraduate seminar series – 2015, where it was evaluated by a panel of physicists of the Department of Physics, University of Peradeniya.
